About Me

My name is Ronan and I am currently based in Galway, Ireland, which means I get to enjoy the many rain showers that grace the county. I've always been curious about technology for as long as I can remember, using computers since the early age of four so it's always made sense that I would end up in the software industry.


Technology captured my curiosity at that young age, and I've been chasing it for years now, my journey led me to the Institute of Technology Sligo where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computing with a specialisation in Games Development. However, I have not just focused on game development, I have also delved into software development and worked on a variety of projects.


My goal is to develop software that makes people's live easier and takes out the tedious parts of their workflow and to develop games that bring a smile to people's face and help them shake the stress of our everday lives. I strive to write modular, clean code that can be reused in a project to help speed up development and reduce the work required.


I have worked on a variety of projects throughout my college course involving virtual reality, RESTful APIs and many other technologies. I'm currently working on a variety of different projects from native android apps to a Unity game and I am looking for job oppurtunities from the Summer of 2021 onwards as I will have completed an additional higher degree in Software Development in the next year.
