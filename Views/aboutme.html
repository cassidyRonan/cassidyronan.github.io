Biography

My name is Ronan and I am a software and game developer currently based in Galway, Ireland, which means I get to enjoy the many rain showers that grace the county. I've recently graduated from the Institute of Technology Sligo with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computing with a specialisation in Games Development and a GPA of 86.6%. I am returning to complete a BSc Honours degree in Software Development for an additional year and will graduate in the Summer of 2021.


However, I have not just focused on game development, I have also delved into software development and worked on a variety of projects. Some of the highlights of my experience would be the development of an inventory management system for an engineering company and a virtual reality based real time strategy game developed in Unity.


My goal is to develop code that is modular, portable and well documented which can be reused in projects to help speed up development and reduce the work required. I have carried out multiple projects using an agile methodology with teams in the past and also acted as a scrum master, most of my experience is with scrum though I have also used kanban.


I have worked on a variety of projects throughout my college course involving virtual reality, RESTful APIs and many other technologies. I'm currently working on a variety of different projects from native android apps to a Unity game and I am looking for job oppurtunities from the Summer of 2021 onwards as I will have completed an additional higher degree in Software Development in the next year.
