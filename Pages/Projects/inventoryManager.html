Project Summary

Year: 2019
Project Status: Completed
Project Duration: 3 Months
Team Size: 1
Software Utilised: Visual Studio 2017, Clip Studio Paint, Trello
Languages Utilised:   C#, Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF), JSON

As part of my work at Advanced NDT Solutions in Co. Westmeath, Ireland, I developed a Windows Presentation Foundation application for the management of stock and equipment within the company. This software allowed for the tracking of equipment and stock amounts of items utilised in the day to day operations of the business.


The project was carried out utilising agile methodologies, particulary the use of Scrum techniques and also with object-oriented design patterns. When the project was first developed back in 2019, I had limited experience with WPF, however, now that I have more experience I am currently working on an updated version of the software with more efficient object-oriented design and an overhauled UI/UX.
