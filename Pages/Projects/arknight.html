Project Summary

Year: 2020 - 2021
Project Status: In Progress
Project Duration: 7 Months
Team Size: 1
Software Utilised: Unity 3D, Asset Forge, Blender, Clip Studio Paint, Trello, Unity AR, AR Core
Languages Utilised:   C#

Google Play Link: Coming Soon


AR Knight: Time Fracture is an augmented reality mobile game on the Android platform where you must stop the evil Prof. Henderson from causing rips in space and time to create true chaos. The game was developed as part of my Project 400 module during my BSc (Honours) in Software Development which I used as an oppurtunity to explore the use of augmented reality in video games and how objects/ models can be interacted with in an immersive 3-Dimensional space.


Using my knowledge gained during my time at the Institute of Technology Sligo, I was able to develop an early release of the game which is intended to be released in Q4 of 2021 for free on the Google Play Store. A large majority of the project's development time was spent integrating the Google Play Platform into the game with features such as cloud saves, google play login and google play achievements.
