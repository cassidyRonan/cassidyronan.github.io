Post Title - 01/01/0001

Hello! My name is Ronan Cassidy and I am fascinated with how code can be used to solve a variety of problems, it's likely due to my love of puzzles. My coding journey began back in 2012 when I began attending a local Coderdojo and was introduced to Scratch, along the way I've learned various languages, agile development and even how to utilise cloud infrastructure.


Along my journey, I have graduated from the Institute of Technology Sligo with a BSc in Games Development at the top of my class, recieveing the Prize Winner Award for the highest GPA in the class and then again with a BSc (Honours) in Software Development a year later. In that time I have also developed an inventory management application for use in the engineering industry by myself as requested by a client.


These days I am currently on the hunt for a software development position which provides an oppurtunity to learn and develop new and old skills alike while solving problems with innovative software solutions. I pride myself on my ability to learn new technologies quickly to utilise them in the project I am working on, I may not know it all but I am prepared to learn it all.
